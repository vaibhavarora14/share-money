Delete your ShareMoney account

Last updated: November 29, 2025

This page explains how to request deletion of your account and associated data on ShareMoney.

How to request deletion

  1. In‑app: Open ShareMoney → Settings → Account → Delete Account. Confirm the prompt.
  2. Or by email: Send an email to varora1406@gmail.com from your registered email with the subject “Delete Account”.
  3. Identity verification: We may ask you to verify via a one‑time code if you email us from a different address.

Processing time: We process deletion requests within 30 days. You’ll receive a confirmation once completed.

What will be deleted

  • Account profile: name, email, phone (if provided), profile photo.
  • App content: your groups, expenses you created, notes, and attachments you uploaded.
  • Device & diagnostics: crash logs and app event logs linked to your account.

What may be retained

  • Billing records: purchase and subscription records from Google Play may be retained for up to 90 days for accounting, fraud prevention, and legal compliance.
  • Aggregated data: non‑identifiable analytics may be kept for product improvement.

Shared group data

Deleting your account removes your membership and entries. Other members in shared groups will still see balances and entries necessary for their records, but your personal identifiers are removed or anonymized.

Reactivation

Account deletion is permanent. If you return later, you’ll need to create a new account.

Need help?

Email varora1406@gmail.com. Please include your registered email and any relevant group names to speed things up.
