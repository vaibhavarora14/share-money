Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 29, 2025

ShareMoney helps groups track shared expenses, split bills, and manage balances. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your choices. If you are under 13, you may not use ShareMoney.

Information We Collect

  • Account data: name, email, phone (optional), profile photo (optional), authentication IDs.
  • App content: groups, members you add, expense entries, amounts, currencies, notes, attachments you upload.
  • Device & logs: app version, device model, OS, crash logs, diagnostics.
  • Payments (if applicable): product IDs, transaction status from Google Play. We do not store full card details.
  • Contacts (optional): invitation emails/phones you provide to invite friends; we don’t sync your address book without consent.

How We Use Data

  • Core features: create accounts, sync groups/expenses, calculate splits and balances, multi‑currency conversions.
  • Communication: send confirmations, invites, reminders, and important notices.
  • Safety & integrity: prevent fraud, secure accounts, enforce terms.
  • Improvement: fix crashes, measure performance, develop new features.

Data Sharing

  • Service providers: cloud hosting, analytics, email/SMS, crash reporting—only to perform services for us.
  • Legal & safety: to comply with law, protect rights, or prevent harm.
  • Business transfers: as part of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, with continued protection.

We do not sell personal data.

Data Storage & Security

Data is encrypted in transit and at rest where applicable. Access is limited and audited. No method is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

Your Rights & Choices

  • Access, correction, deletion: request via email below.
  • Export: request an export of your expense data.
  • Marketing: opt out via email footer or in‑app settings.
  • Permissions: disable optional permissions in device settings.

Children’s Privacy

ShareMoney is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their data. If we learn of such data, we will delete it.

Data Retention

  • Account and group data retained while your account is active.
  • Deleted or anonymized after account closure, subject to legal requirements.
  • Logs and diagnostics retained for a limited period.

International Transfers

Data may be processed in regions outside your country. We apply appropriate safeguards where required.

Third‑Party Services

  • Google Play Billing: purchases and subscriptions.
  • Crash/analytics tools: stability and performance improvements.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy and will notify you of material changes in‑app or via email. Continued use means you accept the updated policy.
